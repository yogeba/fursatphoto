## Meghalaya

### Classics (High Traffic, Some Competition)

| Market | Priority | Notes |
|--------|----------|-------|
| Shillong | High | 243 Airbnb listings, 30% occupancy, ADR $45. Capital city hub. |
| Cherrapunji / Sohra | High | 20 listings, 20% occupancy. Root bridges, caves, Nohkalikai Falls. |
| Dawki | High | Crystal-clear Umngot River. Many day-trippers, few stay-over options. |
